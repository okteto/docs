Development Environments let you code locally on your machine while Okteto keeps your application on Kubernetes updated **in real time**.

The [Okteto Manifest](core/okteto-manifest.mdx) pre-configures your environment so you spend less time on setup and more time writing code.

- [Development images](development/images.mdx) for customizing your container setup
