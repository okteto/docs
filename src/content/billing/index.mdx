# Billing and Licensing

Okteto follows a familiar model for developer tools: per-seat, floating, annual. This section explains exactly what you pay for, how seats are counted, and how to manage your subscription.

## Quick answers

- **You pay per seat**, not per environment, agent, or workload.
- **Seats are floating.** Reassign a seat to a new email any time at no additional cost.
- **New accounts have a 45-day grace window.** Hackathons, pilots, and short-lived evaluations don't count toward your bill, as long as you delete the accounts within 45 days of creation.
- **Your seat usage is visible** in the [Admin Dashboard](admin/dashboard.mdx) and via the [Okteto API](admin/okteto-api.mdx).

For the full details, see [How Billing Works](billing/how-billing-works.mdx).
